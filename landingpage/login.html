About Train of Thought


By: Amor Andal, Angel Alexander, Giovanna Arizpe, & Bianca Martinez

The Train of Thought is a facial recognition of emotions based on facial expresssions captured by the camera on the device. Due to the pandemic many people may be going through a lot of emotions especially with the Covid-19 scares and restrictions when stepping out of your home.

Emotions


Happiness

feeling joyful and showing how cheerful you are with a smile.


Sadness

feeling unhappy and showing sorrow.


Surprised

An expected feeling of an event that happened.


In Love

Showing affection and/or concern for someone.


Corny

A very shy and happy feeling from someone that is meaningful to you or being put on the spotlight


Anger

An emotion of annoyance and madness.


Flirtatious

Behavior towards someone that you are attracted to.


Fear

A feeling of of someone or something being dangerous to you.


Disgusted

Expression that you do NOT approve on in any way.
